Our Mission

We aim to cultivate interest in quantum software and develop the quantum information science community at UCI. We are dedicated to empowering students with the skills to design and implement quantum algorithms on quantum computers. Additionally, we aim to foster strong collaboration within and beyond our community by facilitating interdisciplinary research that combines quantum computing with various fields such as machine learning and optimization.
