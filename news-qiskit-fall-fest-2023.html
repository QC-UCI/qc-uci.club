Qiskit Fall Fest 2023 — Community, Sprints, Momentum

By QC@UCI Communications • November 2023

Qiskit Fall Fest 2023 brought together students across majors to code, learn, and ship small wins. Lightning talks kicked off each sprint; mentors then paired with teams to get ideas off the ground quickly.

We emphasized approachable goals: circuits you can explain, demos you can show, and readmes others can reuse. Several teams continued their builds after the Fest, eventually presenting at club meetings.
