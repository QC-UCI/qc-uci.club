Smarter Traffic with Quantum RL

We're building quantum reinforcement learning agents to optimize traffic lights in real time, improving city flow and reducing congestion. We explore quantum annealing, QAOA, and hybrid methods to control intersections adaptively using real-world data.

Quantum CNN for Self-Driving Cars

We're experimenting with hybrid deep learning networks using quantum circuits to predict vehicle steering angles from road images. This blends classical CNN layers with quantum variational circuits, bringing quantum learning to real-world driving AI.
