Quantum Computing Simulator

We are building a quantum computing simulator from scratch, beginning from the basics—qubits, circuits, and visualizations. This open-source project is a software development project involving the frontend and backend design. The goal is to learn quantum computing and leverage your coding skills simulatenously. If you want to join the project, please fill out this Project Interest Form.

Quantum CNN for Self-Driving Cars

We created a hybrid quantum-classical Convolutional Neural Network to predict vehicle steering angles from road images. Our architecture blends quantum variational circuits with classical optimization algorithms, merging AI and quantum computing for real-world self-driving. Feel free to check out our presentation in the Symposium on Quantum Undergraduate Inquiry & Discovery (S.Q.U.I.D. 2023).

Smart Traffic with a Quantum Optimization Algorithm

We have been exploring well-known quantum algorithms like QAOA and Annealing to optimize traffic lights in real time, improving city flow and reducing congestion.
